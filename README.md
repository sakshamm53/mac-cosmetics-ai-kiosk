# M.A.C. Cosmetics AI Recommendation Kiosk

A product strategy and project management initiative designing a CNN-based facial mapping try-on kiosk for M.A.C. Cosmetics.

## Project Overview
This project was executed as part of a **Project Management** course. I served as the Project Analytics Lead, driving the business case, feasibility modeling, and operational roadmap for an AI-powered retail kiosk. 

The project was managed end-to-end using Agile Scrum methodologies to coordinate deliverables and ensure timeline adherence.

## Project Management & Agile Methodologies
* **Agile Scrum Framework**: Orchestrated project sprints, defining roles and managing deliverables across structured iteration cycles.
* **Trello Board Management**: Structured backlog grooming, task prioritization, and progress tracking using Trello.
* **Sprints & Milestone Tracking**: Participated in daily standups and sprint planning sessions to increase project coordination efficiency by **15%**.

## Key Deliverables
* **MAC_Cosmetics_AI_Kiosk_Presentation.pdf**: The final project presentation detailing the technology stack (CNN), business case, target demographics, financial feasibility, and implementation roadmap.

## Product & Business Impact (Forecasted)
* **Sales Growth**: Projected a **40% increase in sales** via personalized product matchmaking.
* **Customer Engagement**: Modeled a **20% lift in customer engagement** time.
* **Return Rate Reduction**: Forecasted a **21% decrease in product returns** by improving color-match accuracy.

## Tools & Methodologies Used
* **Project Management Tools**: Agile, Scrum, Trello, Sprint Planning, Milestone tracking
* **Business Analytics**: Financial Feasibility Modeling, Target Market Analysis, ROI Forecasting
* **Technology Stacks Modeled**: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for facial mapping
